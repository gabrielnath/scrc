Mission Statement

The Strategic Crisis Response Command coordinates a set of specialized divisions organized around a single principle: some problems cannot wait for jurisdiction to be sorted out first. SCRC exists to remove that delay.

Divisions operate with varying degrees of public visibility depending on function. Some maintain public-facing portals for coordination with field personnel and affiliated agents. Others do not, and are not required to explain why.
